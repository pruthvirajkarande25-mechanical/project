What is Heat Transfer?

Heat transfer is the movement of heat energy from a higher temperature body to a lower temperature body.

Modes of Heat Transfer

  • Conduction
  • Convection
  • Radiation

Fourier’s Law

q = k × A × (T1 - T2) / L

Where:

  • q = Heat Flow Rate
  • k = Thermal Conductivity
  • A = Area
  • T1 = Hot Temperature
  • T2 = Cold Temperature
  • L = Length
